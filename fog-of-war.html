fog of war

about

fog of war is a dynamic website where users can talk about the mystery genre. users can organize their theories with a ck editor and follow threads they like!

stats

started: jun 2023

type: dynamic website

status: ongoing

live link: fog of war

technology used: Python | Flask | ckEditor | JavaScript | react.js | html/css | redux |

splashpage

when entering the site you can choose what form of media you are interseted in to discuss your theories and topics

currently:

  • only the anime and visual novel link is available but i am looking into creating data for each category!
  • the site has thread bumping capability so the most active threads are shown first!
  • the site has breadcrumbs that helps navigate through the site
ckeditor

users can organize their thoughts with the ckeditor

currently:

  • the ckeditor outputs html tags to format the text correctly
  • to prevent xss i sanitize any input being sent to any post requests
follow-threads

if a thread interests you then you can follow it by pressing the heart on the thread!

currently:

  • users can also unfollow threads if they are not interested anymore
  • thread lists are ordered by the last updated post
  • there is nothing implemented to show you already follow a thread
